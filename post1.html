REINCARNATED AS THE STRONGEST MAGICIAN FROM THE WEAKEST HOUSEHOLD

Manga Play Directed By LEON JR

This story is about a boy named Tsukasa who always failed in what he was doing in his world no matter how small it was. On a faithful day, Tsukasa was caught in a road accident where he lost his life—or so he thought.

He was spoken to by a Goddess who gave him requirements and reincarnated him as the strongest sage and knight from the weakest household in the world.

There he was reborn to Baron Eren and Mistress Kimiko and was given the name Loid. He also had a senior sister named Ino.

Loid spent his childhood studying magic without the knowledge of his parents, and privately trained swordsmanship by himself so he could prove he was not a failure in this world.

One day, his father caught him training and renounced him, saying there was no need to train since their household was the weakest and seen as trash. But Loid replied:

“Father, I will make it known to everyone in this village that we can also be the strongest. I will be the strongest mage ever. I promise you.”

Those words shocked his father, who decided to hire a great magical mentor for Loid, while Loid also trained swordsmanship with the Baron’s Knight.

After six years of training, Loid reached the age of 16 and went to the School for Future Mages and Knights.

During the magical aptitude test, Loid touched the crystal orb and emitted a mixture of black, gold, and green light—something unseen since the last hero who was killed by Demon Lord Igris.

Teaching began the following day.
