REINCARNATED AS THE STRONGEST MAGICIAN FROM THE WEAKEST HOUSEHOLD

Manga Play Directed By LEON JR

This story is about a boy named Tsukasa who always failed in what he was doing in his world no matter how small it was. On a faithful day, Tsukasa was caught in a road accident where he lost his life—or so he thought.

He was spoken to by a Goddess who reincarnated him as the strongest sage and knight from the weakest household in the world.

He was reborn as Loid, son of Baron Eren and Mistress Kimiko, with a senior sister named Ino.

Loid secretly trained magic and swordsmanship, determined to prove he was not a failure.

At the academy, Loid awakened a rare aptitude combining black, gold, and green magic—something unseen since the last hero.
